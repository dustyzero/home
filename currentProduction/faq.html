Q: How do I download Python?
A: Go to python.org and download your preferred version. NOTE: SOME FEATURES MAY NOT WORK WITH OLDER VERSIONS OF PYTHON!

Q: How do I unzip the folder?
A: Download an application that will unzip folders for you. (You can find my recommended application by searching '7zip' on Google or DuckDuckGo). Once you have downloaded 7zip you can right click the folder and extract to 'Press enter to begin/' or 'Coded Clash/', etc. All of these instructions can be found on the game's itch.io page.
